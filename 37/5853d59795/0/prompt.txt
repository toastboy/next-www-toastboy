Bootstrapping yearPlayerRecords/allTimePlayerRecords with full Prisma PlayerRecord rows risks carrying non-writable/read-only fields (e.g., id, timestamps) into later writes (the map contents are eventually cast to PlayerRecordWriteInput in calculateYearPlayerRecords). To avoid Prisma validation errors or unintended updates, store only the writable fields needed for the upsert (or explicitly omit read-only columns) when seeding these maps.

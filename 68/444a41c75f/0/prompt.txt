The (rest as { accountEmail?: ... }) cast bypasses type-safety and can mask a real data issue (e.g. accountEmail not actually present on the fetched shape, leading to it always being null). Prefer making accountEmail part of the Prisma result type explicitly (via a typed select/include that actually fetches it) or deriving it in a clearly-typed way (e.g. from a relation), and avoid the cast so TypeScript can enforce correctness.

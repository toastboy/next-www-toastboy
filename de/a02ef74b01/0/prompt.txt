Research and Development Insight
Tokenovate Limited is engaged in developing methods for creating digital tokens linked to physical assets, as described in 'Geo-linked token,' which details generating digital tokens for land parcels with associated attributes, and in 'Rateable token,' which outlines applying ratings to digital tokens representing assets, including metadata such as location and asset type. These activities focus on digital representation and evaluation of real-world assets.

Product Description
 
Tokenovate Platform    A cloud-based platform automating post-trade lifecycle events for derivatives and securities financing transactions using blockchain and smart contracts.
Unified Trade Record    A standardized, immutable on-chain record consolidating all trade data into a single authoritative source for transaction accuracy.

# Migrating to the New Footy Site

Welcome to the new toastboy.co.uk footy site. This document covers what has changed
from the old site and what you need to do to get started.

## Signing in

The old site used a simple email-based login. The new site uses **OAuth via Google
or Microsoft**, so you sign in with whichever of those accounts matches the email
address we already have on record for you.

If neither of those options suits you, you can request a magic-link sign-in by
email instead. Use the **Sign in** button on the home page and choose
**Continue with email**.

## Your player profile

Your stats, history, and league records have all been carried over automatically.
Your player page is at `/footy/player/<your-name>`.

If anything looks wrong — missing games, incorrect scores, or a name that is not
quite right — let Jon know and he will fix it.

## Responding to game invitations

The process is the same as before: you will receive an email before each game and
can click the link to say whether you are in or out.

On the new site you can also respond directly from your player page or from the
[next game](/footy/game) page without needing the email link.

## Ratings

You still rate other players after each game. The ratings page is linked from the
[results](/footy/results) page once the game record is published.

## What is gone

- The old `/footy/mailout` URL redirects to `/footy/players`.
- The old `/footy/nextgame` URL redirects to `/footy/game`.
- The old points/pub/stalwart/speedy table URLs all redirect to their new
  equivalents under `/footy/table/`.
- The admin pages (picker, new game, new player, responses) have moved under
  `/footy/admin/` and are only visible if you have admin access.
